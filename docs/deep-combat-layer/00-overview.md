# Deep Combat Layer

The Deep Combat Layer (DCL) adapts the physical combat structure of GURPS 4e to the grid, turn
economy, equipment roster, and heroic progression of Final Fantasy Tactics. It uses GURPS as a
balance framework rather than as a promise to reproduce every tabletop subsystem.

The physical DCL has five layers, resolved in order:

1. attributes produce derived characteristics;
2. CT establishes initiative and a unit receives Movement plus Action;
3. an attack tests the relevant DX-based skill;
4. the defender receives one legal active defense unless the attack is critical or undefendable;
5. damage penetrates the applicable equipment DR and becomes injury.

The document map below is the rules index. Scope exclusions, deliberate FFT adaptations, retired
legacy fields, and the boundary with the future magic layer are owned by
[Scope, Calibration, and Retired Fields](10-scope-calibration-and-retired-fields.md).

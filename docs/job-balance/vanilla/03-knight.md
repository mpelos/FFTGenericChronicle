# Knight (Vanilla / IVC)

*Classic: Knight · Command: Arts of War (Rend) · Stats (mult.): HP 120 · MP 80 · Speed 100 · PA 120 · MA 80 · Move 3 · Jump 3 · C-Ev 10%*

The armored melee chassis: high HP and PA, heavy armor and shield access, and a command built around
destroying or shredding the enemy's equipment and stats.

## End-game tier: B  (as offense alone: C)

**Community read.** Knight is the sturdy early tank and a reliable physical body, but its *Arts of
War / Rend* skills are seen as situational — strong control against equipment-bearing human enemies,
useless against monsters, and largely redundant once your damage snowballs. Its enduring value is as
a **durable chassis** and as the donor of **Equip Heavy Armor / Equip Shields**.

**Why this tier.** A great base and a great support source, a mediocre destination. The Rend toolkit
is accuracy-gated denial that fixes specific problems (disarm a dangerous boss, strip a healer's
gear) rather than carrying fights, and raw damage jobs out-scale it late.

## Strengths

- **Best baseline durability of the starter physicals** — HP 120, 10% C-Ev, and access to heavy
  armor + shields; the natural tank body.
- **High PA 120** — solid melee damage with a strong weapon even though the command isn't its
  damage engine.
- **Permanent equipment destruction** — Rend Weapon/Armor/Shield can defang gear-dependent enemies
  for the rest of the battle.

## Weaknesses

- **Rend does nothing to monsters or already-stripped foes**, and every Rend is a physical
  hit-chance roll — whiff-prone against evasive targets.
- **Slow and grounded** — Move 3, no mobility or reaction contribution of its own.
- **No magic and no scaling command** — as pure offense it is outclassed by Monk, Ninja, Samurai.

## Action skills — Arts of War (Rend)

| Skill | Effect | Design read |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Rend Helm / Rend Armor / Rend Shield / Rend Weapon | Destroy target's headgear / body / shield / weapon | Permanent gear denial; great vs equipment-reliant humans/bosses, dead vs monsters; gated by hit chance. |
| Rend MP | Drain/reduce target MP | Anti-caster pressure; slow way to starve a mage. |
| Rend Speed | Lower target Speed | Tempo control; meaningful on a key threat, marginal otherwise. |
| Rend Power / Rend Magick | Lower target Physical / Magick Attack | Damage suppression on a heavy hitter; situational softening. |

## Reaction / Support / Movement (cross-job value)

Knight's lasting contribution to the build economy is its equipment-access supports.

- **Support — Equip Heavy Armor:** unlock heavy armor (large HP/defense) on any job. The premier
  durability splash; turns a fragile hybrid into a wall.
- **Support — Equip Shields:** unlock a shield (flat physical/magic evade %) on any job. Cheap, broad
  survivability for any front-line build.
- **Reaction / Movement:** nothing native of note — Knight is a chassis and a support donor, not a
  reaction/mobility source.

## Build relevance

Field Knight as a **tanky base** for a strong secondary (e.g., Knight body + Items, or + a magic
command it can afford), or strip **Equip Heavy Armor / Equip Shields** onto another job that needs to
survive. The Rend command is a situational anti-boss/anti-human toolbox you bring on purpose, not a
default. A chassis and a donor more than a star.

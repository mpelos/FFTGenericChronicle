# Archer (Vanilla / IVC)

*Classic: Archer · Command: Aim (Charge) · Stats (mult.): HP 100 · MP 65 · Speed 100 · PA 110 · MA 80 · Move 3 · Jump 3 · C-Ev 10%*

The ranged physical job: bows, height, and a command that trades turns for a bigger single hit. Its
own command ages poorly, but it is the gateway to one of the best supports in the game.

## End-game tier: C  (its Concentration support: A)

**Community read.** Archer is regarded as a weak fielded job. The *Aim/Charge* mechanic is slow and
telegraphed — you spend turns winding up while faster enemies reposition or kill you — and its damage
is matched by jobs that act every turn. What players actually level Archer for is **Concentration**:
ignore-evasion is a top-tier support that fixes accuracy for every physical attacker.

**Why this tier.** As a primary it pays tempo for damage at a bad exchange rate. The job is a
stepping-stone to a great export, not a destination.

## Strengths

- **Solid PA (110) at range** with bow reach and height bonuses — safe poke from the back/high ground.
- **Aim stacks a large single hit** from a position the enemy can't immediately punish.
- **Donates Concentration** — ignore enemy evasion, the answer to evasion/Transparent-heavy fights.

## Weaknesses

- **Charge is slow and telegraphed** — you forfeit tempo, and a target that moves can be missed; the
  opportunity cost almost always beats the payoff.
- **No magic, low MP, Move 3** — limited reach of influence; grounded.
- **Bow output is height/line dependent** — situational compared to a melee that just acts.

## Action skills — Aim (Charge)

| Skill | Effect | Design read |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Aim +1 / +2 | Short-charge shot, small bonus | The only practical tiers — minimal delay for a modest bump. |
| Aim +3 / +4 / +5 / +7 | Medium charge, scaling damage | Diminishing returns: the delay starts to cost more than the extra damage. |
| Aim +10 / +20 | Long / longest charge, big damage | Big numbers, impractical timing; setup/cheese or finishing a stationary target only. |

## Reaction / Support / Movement (cross-job value)

Archer's portable export is the real reason to visit it.

- **Support — Concentration (ignore evasion):** physical attacks land regardless of evade, Transparent,
  or facing. Near auto-include on any dedicated physical DPS in an evasion-heavy game; this is Archer's
  best-in-class contribution.
- **Reaction — Speed Save (≈ Speed Surge: raise Speed when hit):** snowball tempo as you take damage;
  decent on a durable unit, situational otherwise.
- **Reaction — Arrow Guard (≈ Archer's Bane: evade ranged):** hard counter to archer/gun-heavy maps;
  narrow but strong where it applies.

## Build relevance

A weak primary, a mandatory stop for **Concentration** — which you graft onto Ninja, Monk, or any
melee that needs its hits to connect. Field Archer only for safe-corner poke; otherwise mine the
support and move on.

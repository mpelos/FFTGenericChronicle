# White Mage (Vanilla / IVC)

*Classic: White Mage · Command: White Magicks · Stats (mult.): HP 80 · MP 120 · Speed 110 · PA 90 · MA 110 · Move 3 · Jump 3 · C-Ev 5%*

The dedicated healer and buffer: scaling cures, full revival, the Protect/Shell/Wall suite, cleanse,
and a holy nuke. Proactive support, where Chemist is reactive.

## End-game tier: B  (a strong support; capped by its competition)

**Community read.** White Mage is the reliable backbone healer — fast (Speed 110), with a complete
toolkit. Its ceiling is held down by *who else can heal*: Chemist items are Faith- and MP-free, and
Monk's Chakra/Revive cost nothing, so raw curing isn't unique to it. Where White Mage stays
best-in-class is the **buff suite** — party-wide Protect/Shell, Wall, and especially **Reraise** —
and dependable mass revival.

**Why this tier.** Indispensable in the abstract, but its healing overlaps with cheaper options, and
it is Faith-gated and charge-timed. The buffs and Reraise are what justify the slot late.

## Strengths

- **Complete proactive support:** scaling heals, Raise/Arise revival, Reraise auto-revive, Regen,
  Protect/Shell + party-wide Protectja/Shellja, Wall, and Esuna cleanse — every defensive need in one
  command.
- **Speed 110 + MA 110:** acts often and, at high Faith, heals and buffs land hard.
- **Holy:** a strong single-target holy nuke for when support turns to offense.

## Weaknesses

- **Faith-gated and charge-timed:** low-Faith allies heal poorly, and the big spells have CT windows
  where a fast enemy interrupts or kills the caster mid-cast.
- **Fragile:** HP 80, robes, C-Ev 5% — must be protected.
- **Healing overlaps Chemist/Monk** — its curing isn't unique, so it competes for its own slot.

## Action skills — White Magicks

| Skill | Effect | Design read |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Cure / Cura / Curaga / Curaja | Restore small → massive HP | The healing spine; Faith- and CT-scaled, single or small AoE. |
| Raise / Arise | Revive (partial / full HP) | Reliable revival; Arise brings a unit back battle-ready. |
| Reraise | Grant auto-revive status | Best-in-class insurance; pre-cast it and a death undoes itself. |
| Regen | Gradual HP regen | Efficient sustained healing on a frontliner. |
| Protect / Protectja | Physical defense (single / party) | Party-wide Protectja is a premier opening buff. |
| Shell / Shellja | Magick defense (single / party) | The anti-caster mirror of Protect; party version is key vs mages. |
| Wall | Protect + Shell together | One-button full mitigation on a key unit. |
| Esuna | Cure several statuses | The proactive cleanse (Faith-gated, unlike items). |
| Holy | Heavy holy magick damage | Strong nuke; expensive, and resisted/absorbed by holy-aligned foes. |

## Reaction / Support / Movement (cross-job value)

White Mage's value is its command; the portable slots that matter to *any* caster build cluster
around economy and survival.

- **Reaction — Regenerate (gain Regen when hit):** keeps a squishy caster alive through chip; a clean
  fit on a back-liner.
- **Support — caster economy (Halve MP / a charge-time cut):** any spell job wants cheaper, faster
  casts; White Mage's big-MP heals especially benefit.
- **Movement — Manafont (≈ Move-MP Up: regen MP by moving):** tops up the MP pool just by
  repositioning — strong on a long-fight healer.

## Build relevance

The default proactive support primary, and a buff/revival anchor for slow, attritional fights. As a
secondary, **White Magicks** gives a durable job party-wide Protect/Shell or emergency Raise. Field it
for the buff suite and Reraise more than for raw healing, which cheaper jobs also cover.

# Black Mage (Vanilla / IVC)

*Classic: Black Mage · Command: Black Magicks · Stats (mult.): HP 75 · MP 120 · Speed 100 · PA 60 · MA 150 · Move 3 · Jump 3 · C-Ev 5%*

The pure nuker: the highest Magick Attack in the roster, three elements across four tiers, plus
non-elemental Flare and the Death/Toad utility spells. Enormous burst, gated by charge time.

## End-game tier: B  (drops toward C the faster the fight)

**Community read.** Black Mage is the iconic glass cannon — top MA and the biggest single-spell
damage in the game — but its reputation is permanently shadowed by **charge time**. In FFT's quick
turn economy, the AoE often arrives after fast enemies have scattered or after the caster is dead.
Against slow foes and tight clusters it is devastating; against mobile, spread enemies it is
unreliable. **A charge-time cut (Short Charge) is close to mandatory** to make it work.

**Why this tier.** The damage is real and the elemental/utility coverage is broad, but reliability —
not power — is the limiter, and late game summons and the Arithmetician's *instant* spells out-deliver
it.

## Strengths

- **Highest MA (150)** → the largest magic hits available; **Flare** is non-elemental and bypasses
  elemental resist.
- **Full elemental coverage (Fire / Lightning / Ice × 4 tiers):** exploit weaknesses, stack with Oil
  for fire, and blanket clusters with AoE.
- **Utility spells:** Death (magical instant KO), Toad (disable), Poison (attrition / undead).

## Weaknesses

- **Charge time is the core flaw:** long CT windows let fast enemies leave the blast or interrupt the
  cast; the wind-up, not the damage, is what fails.
- **Extremely fragile:** HP 75, robes, C-Ev 5%, Speed 100 — dies the moment it's reached.
- **Faith- and element-gated:** damage drops vs low Faith and is resisted or *absorbed* by aligned
  foes; out-scaled late by summons and instant-cast Arithmeticks.

## Reaction / Support / Movement (cross-job value)

Black Mage's power sits in its command; the build around it is about *landing* and *amplifying* casts.

- **Support — Magick Boost (raise magick damage):** straight damage scaling for any caster; the
  natural amplifier for high-MA nukes.
- **Support — Short Charge / Swiftspell (cut charge time):** the reliability fix every charger needs;
  classically a Time Mage export, and what a Black Mage build is built to borrow.
- **Reaction — Magick Counter (retaliate with a spell):** punishes attackers with chip even while the
  caster is busy charging.

## Build relevance

A high-ceiling primary that lives or dies on charge time — pair **Black Magicks** with a charge-time
cut and protect the caster's position. As a secondary it bolts burst onto an MA-statted job, but the
fragility and CT mean it shines in controlled, slow fights rather than fast skirmishes.

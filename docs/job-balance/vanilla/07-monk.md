# Monk (Vanilla / IVC)

*Classic: Monk · Command: Martial Arts · Innate: Brawler · Stats (mult.): HP 135 · MP 80 · Speed 110 · PA 129 · MA 80 · Move 3 · Jump 4 · C-Ev 20%*

The bare-handed bruiser: the highest HP and among the highest PA in the roster, ranged fist attacks,
and MP-free self/team sustain. A self-sufficient damage-and-healing engine.

## End-game tier: A  (many rank it S)

**Community read.** Monk is a consensus top-tier job. Brutal HP and PA, no weapon required (Brawler
makes fists hit full), ranged options to poke from safety, and — crucially — **MP-free healing,
cleanse, and revival**. It is strong from the moment you unlock it and stays excellent to the end. Its
only real knock is light armor, offset by the biggest HP pool and a high innate evade.

**Why this tier.** A unit that out-damages most weapons bare-handed, hits at range, heals itself and
neighbours, and raises the dead — without spending MP or a weapon slot — does almost everything a
front line needs in one job.

## Strengths

- **Best bruiser stat line:** HP 135, PA 129, Speed 110, 20% C-Ev — fast, beefy, hard to pin down.
- **Brawler innate:** full bare-handed damage frees the weapon hand and saves all weapon JP/gil; bare
  hands also carry the high evade.
- **Ranged + AoE fists:** Aurablast/Shockwave poke from distance ignoring weapon evade; Cyclone clears
  everything adjacent.
- **MP-free sustain:** Chakra (HP+MP, self+adjacent), Revive (raise the dead), Purification (cleanse) —
  a backup medic baked into a DPS.

## Weaknesses

- **Light armor only** — low raw mitigation; leans on HP and evade, so magic bursts hurt.
- **Output is roll- and Brave-based** — Pummel is random, and the fist/Chakra formulas scale with
  Brave, so a low-Brave Monk underperforms.
- **No weapon means no weapon procs/elements** — pure physical, no elemental or status riders.

## Action skills — Martial Arts

| Skill | Effect | Design read |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Cyclone | Damage all adjacent tiles | Crowd control when surrounded; great on a diver. |
| Pummel | Multiple random physical hits | High single-target burst, RNG spread. |
| Aurablast | Ranged physical wave | Free ranged poke; ignores weapon evade. |
| Shockwave | Long-range physical line | The classic "ranged nuke on a melee job." |
| Doom Fist | Melee hit + inflict Doom | Situational execution (timed death). |
| Purification | Clear negative statuses (self/area) | MP-free party cleanse. |
| Chakra | Restore HP + MP (self + adjacent) | One of the best sustain buttons in the game; no MP cost. |
| Revive | Raise a KO'd ally | Item/MP-free revival; turns the Monk into a safety net. |

## Reaction / Support / Movement (cross-job value)

- **Support — Brawler (innate):** bare-handed damage scales to full. Defines the unarmed build and
  makes any job's empty hands viable; pairs with the high evade of fighting unarmed.
- **Reaction — Counter:** strike back when hit. With Monk's PA this counter hurts; excellent on a
  front-line body that gets attacked a lot.
- **Reaction — Hamedo (≈ First Strike: pre-empt melee with your own hit):** negates the incoming melee
  entirely — devastating, though very expensive to learn.

## Build relevance

A premier fielded primary *and* a donor. Field Monk as a self-sufficient diver (damage + Chakra +
Revive), or splash **Martial Arts** as a secondary to give any job ranged pokes and MP-free sustain,
or lift **Brawler** onto an unarmed build. One of the strongest, most flexible jobs in the game.

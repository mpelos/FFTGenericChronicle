# Thief (Vanilla / IVC)

*Classic: Thief · Command: Steal · Stats (mult.): HP 90 · MP 50 · Speed 110 · PA 100 · MA 60 · Move 4 · Jump 4 · C-Ev 25%*

The mobility specialist: the fastest, most evasive, most mobile body in the roster, with a command
built around taking things — gear, gil, hearts — rather than dealing damage.

## End-game tier: C  (utility raises it situationally)

**Community read.** Thief is prized for *mobility and theft*, not combat. It has the best Speed,
Move, Jump, and evasion in the game, and Steal can permanently pilfer rare equipment from specific
enemies (the famous use). But Steal deals no damage, its success is gated by a Speed check that often
fails against fast or high-level targets, and the body is fragile. Most players use Thief in bursts —
grab the steals, or run a speedy harasser — then move on.

**Why this tier.** As an end-game fielded combatant it underperforms (no damage command, light armor,
low HP). Its lasting value is the mobility it exports and the niche of acquiring otherwise-unobtainable
gear.

## Strengths

- **Best mobility and evasion in the game:** Speed 110, Move 4, Jump 4, C-Ev 25% — acts often,
  repositions anywhere, and dodges physical attacks outright.
- **Permanent equipment theft:** Steal Weapon/Accessory can grab rare uniques *and* disarm the target.
- **Control & economy:** Steal Heart (Charm) disables or recruits; Steal Gil/EXP bend the economy.

## Weaknesses

- **Steal deals no damage** and is **Speed-gated** — unreliable against faster/higher-level foes, and a
  whiffed steal is a wasted turn.
- **Fragile:** HP 90, light armor only, MA 60 — no mitigation, no magic, weak burst.
- **Most steals are situational** — outside specific gear grabs the command is low-impact.

## Action skills — Steal

| Skill | Effect | Design read |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Steal Gil | Take gil | Trivial economy chip. |
| Steal Heart | Inflict Charm | Strong disable/recruit when it lands; affected by gender/zodiac. |
| Steal Helm / Armor / Shield | Take that piece | Disarms defense and nets gear; accuracy-gated. |
| Steal Weapon | Take the weapon | The prize steal — disarms the threat and grabs rare weapons. |
| Steal Accessory | Take the accessory | Best for rare accessory acquisition. |
| Steal EXP | Drain target EXP | Niche level control. |

## Reaction / Support / Movement (cross-job value)

Thief's enduring export is mobility, and reactions that stack with its huge evade.

- **Movement — Move +2 / Move +3:** the classic high-mobility splash; map control for any build, and
  among the most valuable movement abilities in the game.
- **Reaction — Vigilance / Reflexes (raise evasion when targeted):** compounds Thief's 25% C-Ev into a
  genuine dodge-tank on a light, fast body.
- **Reaction — Sticky Fingers (steal when hit):** opportunistic theft; flavorful, niche.

## Build relevance

A niche primary (grab specific steals, or a fast Charm/harass body) but a premier **mobility donor** —
its Move +2/+3 and evasion reactions graft onto any unit that wants to act first and reposition freely.
Field it on purpose; export its movement everywhere.

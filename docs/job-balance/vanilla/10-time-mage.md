# Time Mage (Vanilla / IVC)

*Classic: Time Mage · Command: Time Magicks · Stats (mult.): HP 75 · MP 120 · Speed 100 · PA 50 · MA 130 · Move 3 · Jump 3 · C-Ev 5%*

The turn-economy controller: Haste, Slow, Stop, and Quick bend who acts and how often, backed by
percent-HP Gravity, Reflect/Float utility, and the home of the best caster support and movement.

## End-game tier: A  (some argue S for Quick + Short Charge)

**Community read.** Time Mage is rated highly not for damage but for **controlling the action
economy** — the most powerful lever in FFT. Haste/Hasteja give your team more turns, Slow/Stop take
the enemy's away, and **Quick** hands an ally an immediate extra turn outright. Add **Gravity**
(percent-HP damage that ignores defense and Faith) for chunking bosses, and the fact that Time Mage is
where **Short Charge** and **Teleport** live, and it underpins most optimized play.

**Why this tier.** Tempo wins tactical fights, and no job manipulates tempo like this one — while also
exporting the two abilities (Short Charge, Teleport) that make every other caster better.

## Strengths

- **Turn-economy control:** Haste/Hasteja, Slow/Slowja, Stop, and especially **Quick** (a free extra
  turn) warp action economy in your favor.
- **Gravity / Graviga:** percent-current-HP magick damage — bypasses defense and Faith to soften
  high-HP targets nothing else dents efficiently.
- **Utility:** Reflect (bounce magic), Float (negate earth/Geomancy/traps), Immobilize, plus Meteor as
  a high-end (if unreliable) AoE.

## Weaknesses

- **Weak direct damage:** Gravity is capped, Meteor's charge is impractical — it controls, it doesn't
  nuke.
- **Fragile and charge-gated:** HP 75, robes, and CT windows on the bigger effects.
- **Faith/CT-gated control:** Stop, Quick, and the -ja spells are expensive and not instant.

## Action skills — Time Magicks

| Skill | Effect | Design read |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Haste / Hasteja | Raise Speed/CT (single / party) | Core tempo buff; party-wide Hasteja is a premier opener. |
| Slow / Slowja | Lower enemy Speed/CT (single / party) | Enemy tempo denial; Slowja can cripple a whole side. |
| Stop | Freeze a target | Removes a unit from the fight entirely if it lands. |
| Quick | Grant an immediate turn | One of the strongest effects in the game — a free action for a key unit. |
| Immobilize / Float | Prevent movement / grant Float | Lockdown and earth/trap negation utility. |
| Reflect | Magic-reflect status | Bounce enemy spells; can be set up on allies cleverly. |
| Gravity / Graviga | Percent-HP magick damage (single / AoE) | Defense/Faith-ignoring softening of high-HP foes. |
| Meteor | Massive AoE magick damage | Huge ceiling, long charge — setup/cheese rather than reliable. |

## Reaction / Support / Movement (cross-job value)

Time Mage's exports are best-in-class and the reason every caster visits it.

- **Support — Short Charge (≈ Swiftspell: cut charge time):** the reliability fix for *every* charged
  caster (Black Mage, Summoner, itself). Near-mandatory on serious magic builds; Time Mage's signature
  donation.
- **Movement — Teleport:** move anywhere on the map. The single best movement ability — repositions a
  fragile caster out of danger or onto a perfect angle.
- **Reaction — Mana Shield (spend MP to absorb damage):** turns a big MP pool into an extra health bar
  on a squishy caster.

## Build relevance

A top control primary *and* the premier support/movement donor. Field **Time Magicks** for Haste/Quick
and Slow lockdown; lift **Short Charge** onto any charger and **Teleport** onto anyone who needs to be
somewhere now. One of the most build-defining jobs in the game.

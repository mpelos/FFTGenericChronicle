# Summoner (Vanilla / IVC)

*Classic: Summoner · Command: Summon · Stats (mult.): HP 70 · MP 125 · Speed 90 · PA 50 · MA 125 · Move 3 · Jump 3 · C-Ev 5%*

The area caster: wide-area elemental damage and healing for a fixed cost, the cheap mid-tier summons
that carry the early game, and Golem — a party-wide physical barrier that defines the job.

## End-game tier: A  (some say B)

**Community read.** Summoner delivers the biggest *areas* in the game and does it efficiently: the
mid-tier summons (Shiva/Ramuh/Ifrit/Titan) cover every element at low JP and one button hits a whole
cluster. **Golem** is the standout — a barrier that soaks physical damage for the entire party — and
Moogle/Faerie add AoE healing. It shares Black Magic's charge-time problem and is even more fragile,
but its area reach and Golem push it above the pure nuker.

**Why this tier.** Efficient area output plus a top-tier defensive tool (Golem) in one command, with
the highest MP pool to fuel it; the fragility and charge time are the price.

## Strengths

- **Efficient wide AoE:** the cheap mid summons (Shiva/Ramuh/Ifrit/Titan) give full elemental coverage
  for little JP and blanket clusters; high-end summons (Bahamut/Odin/Zodiark) finish.
- **Golem:** a party-wide barrier that absorbs physical damage until depleted — one of the strongest
  defensive plays available.
- **AoE healing and buffs:** Moogle/Faerie heal an area; Carbuncle grants a defensive status; Lich
  drains.

## Weaknesses

- **Charge time + low Speed (90):** the slowest caster start, with interruptible wind-ups — the same
  reliability tax as Black Magic, worse tempo.
- **Most fragile in the roster:** HP 70, robes, C-Ev 5% — dies instantly if reached.
- **Area hits allies too, and top summons are JP-hungry** (Zodiark 9999) — positioning and investment
  both matter.

## Reaction / Support / Movement (cross-job value)

Summoner's power is its command; the build around it is about fueling and surviving the cast.

- **Support — Halve MP:** the highest-MP job turns this into a doubled casting economy — premier on a
  summoner, valuable on any caster.
- **Support — Short Charge (borrowed from Time Mage):** the charge-time fix its slow summons need to
  land before the cluster scatters.
- **Movement — Manafont (≈ Move-MP Up):** regenerate MP by repositioning, sustaining long summon
  chains.

## Build relevance

A strong area primary and a defensive utility carrier (Golem alone earns the slot). As a secondary,
**Summon** bolts wide AoE or a party barrier onto an MA-statted body. Pair it with a charge-time cut
and keep it protected — its reach and Golem are worth the babysitting.

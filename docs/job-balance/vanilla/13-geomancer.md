# Geomancer (Vanilla / IVC)

*Classic: Geomancer · Command: Geomancy · Stats (mult.): HP 110 · MP 95 · Speed 100 · PA 110 · MA 105 · Move 4 · Jump 3 · C-Ev 10%*

The terrain warrior: free, instant, ranged elemental attacks with a status rider, on a sturdy hybrid
body with good gear and Move 4. The quintessential jack-of-all-trades.

## End-game tier: B  (a reliable all-rounder)

**Community read.** Geomancer is respected as a dependable do-everything job. Geomancy costs no MP,
has no charge time, hits at range, scales with PA, and tacks a status onto the damage — repeatable
output with no resource cost. Its stats are well-rounded (good HP/PA/MA, Move 4) and its equipment is
solid (swords + robes), making it a durable hybrid. The knock is the opposite of a flaw and a virtue
at once: it is good at everything and best at nothing, and its attack is dictated by the terrain.

**Why this tier.** Reliability and flexibility without a peak — a strong, low-maintenance contributor
that specialists out-perform in their lanes.

## Strengths

- **Free, instant, ranged damage + status:** no MP, no charge, PA-scaling, with a control rider — the
  most resource-efficient repeatable attack among the casters/hybrids.
- **Sturdy hybrid chassis:** HP 110, PA 110, MA 105, Move 4, 10% C-Ev, with sword + robe access — it
  survives and repositions well.
- **Built-in control:** the status riders (slow/sleep/etc., terrain-dependent) add lockdown on top of
  damage.

## Weaknesses

- **Terrain-dependent:** the available attack — and its element — is chosen by the tile, so barren or
  uniform ground gives weak or repetitive options you don't control.
- **Resistible:** the element can be resisted or absorbed, and the status rider isn't guaranteed.
- **Master of none:** out-damaged by dedicated nukers and out-tanked by dedicated bodies.

## Action skills — Geomancy

> All Geomancy skills are one mechanic: a ranged elemental attack (damage + status rider) whose
> identity is set by the tile the Geomancer stands on.

| Skill (by terrain) | Effect | Design read |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Sinkhole / Tremor / Quicksand / Sandstorm (earth/ground) | Ranged damage + status | The "what tile am I on" attack — free, instant, PA-scaling. |
| Torrent / Snowstorm (water/ice) | Ranged damage + status | Element follows terrain; exploit weaknesses where the map allows. |
| Tanglevine / Will-o'-the-Wisp / Magma Surge (plant/fire) | Ranged damage + status | Same engine, different element/rider per tile. |
| Wind Slash / Wind Blast / Contortion (air/other) | Ranged damage + status | Rounds out coverage; reliability depends on the battlefield. |

## Reaction / Support / Movement (cross-job value)

- **Support — Attack Boost (raise physical damage):** a straight damage multiplier for any physical
  build; one of the more valuable offensive supports, classically in Geomancer's kit.
- **Movement — Ignore Terrain / Any Ground:** move freely over the very terrain Geomancy reads;
  utility mobility for any unit on rough maps.
- **Reaction:** nothing signature — Geomancer's value is its command and its supports, not a reaction.

## Build relevance

A solid fielded primary (free ranged damage + control on a tough body) and a useful secondary
(**Geomancy** gives any PA job MP-free ranged pokes). Its **Attack Boost** export lifts every physical
attacker. Field it as a reliable generalist; lift its support onto your damage dealers.

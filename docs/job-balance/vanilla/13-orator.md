# Orator (Vanilla / IVC)

*Classic: Mediator · Command: Speechcraft · Innate: Beast Tongue · Stats (mult.): HP 80 · MP 70 · Speed 100 · PA 75 · MA 75 · Move 3 · Jump 3 · C-Ev 5%*

The talker: recruitment, morale manipulation, and economy. No damage of its own — a pure
utility/social job whose effects are powerful in theory and unreliable in practice.

## End-game tier: C  (mainstream view leans D; a niche specialist)

**Community read.** Orator/Mediator is one of the lowest-regarded jobs. Its talks are slow,
MA/Faith-gated, and frequently *fail*, and several effects (Invite, Condemn) don't work on bosses.
Yet min-maxers know the Brave/Faith levers and **Entice (Invite)** hide real, even degenerate power:
recruit enemies and monsters, permanently bend a target's Brave/Faith, mark a unit for death. The gap
between its theoretical ceiling and its reliable floor is what keeps it niche.

**Why this tier.** Combat-null and unreliable, so it never carries a fight — but the recruitment and
permanent stat-manipulation give it a real, if situational, reason to exist.

## Strengths

- **Brave/Faith manipulation is a genuine lever:** lower enemy Brave (less physical damage, fewer
  reactions), lower enemy Faith (they take/deal less magic), raise ally Faith (stronger heals/nukes) —
  and the changes are permanent.
- **Entice/Invite:** recruit enemy units, and (with innate **Beast Tongue**) monsters — roster and
  collection power no other job has.
- **Condemn:** a death-sentence timer on a tough target.

## Weaknesses

- **Low, MA/Faith-gated success rates** — many talks simply miss; it is slow and turn-expensive.
- **Fragile and damageless** — HP 80, C-Ev 5%, no offense; it can't defend itself.
- **Boss-immune effects** — its flashiest tools (Invite, Condemn) fail exactly where you'd want them.

## Action skills — Speechcraft

| Skill | Effect | Design read |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Entice | Recruit/control target (Invite) | The marquee skill; degenerate with monsters + Beast Tongue, unreliable and boss-immune. |
| Stall | Lower target Speed | Tempo control; useful on a key threat if it lands. |
| Praise / Intimidate | Raise / lower target Bravery | Buff allies' physical, or defang an enemy's damage and reactions; permanent. |
| Preach / Enlighten | Raise / lower target Faith | Pump your casters' targets, or sink an enemy's magic exchange; permanent. |
| Condemn | Death sentence (timed KO) | Strong execution on non-bosses; slow to pay off. |
| Defraud | Gil/economy effect | Money utility; trivial in combat. |
| Insult / Mimic Darlavon | Inflict a status | Status pressure, success-gated and inconsistent. |

## Reaction / Support / Movement (cross-job value)

Orator's exports are thin — its power lives in the command, not in portable slots.

- **Support — Beast Tongue (innate):** communicate with/control monsters; the enabler for monster
  recruiting and monster-team builds. Its one notable splash.
- **Support — Tame:** recruit/control monsters outright; pairs with the above for a beast-master build.
- **Reaction / Movement:** nothing defining — you field Orator for its command, you don't mine it.

## Build relevance

A deliberate niche pick: bring it to recruit, to permanently bend a key enemy's Brave/Faith, or to
build a monster team. It anchors no mainstream build and exports little; its value is the specific
problem only it can solve.

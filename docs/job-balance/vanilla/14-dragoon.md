# Dragoon (Vanilla / IVC)

*Classic: Lancer · Command: Jump · Stats (mult.): HP 120 · MP 50 · Speed 100 · PA 120 · MA 50 · Move 3 · Jump 4 · C-Ev 15%*

The spear leaper: a single command — Jump — that sends the unit off the field to crash down next turn
for heavy single-target damage, untargetable while airborne. Iconic, durable, and tempo-negative.

## End-game tier: C  (some rate B for the safe-damage niche)

**Community read.** Dragoon's reputation outruns its performance in the classic meta. Jump deals big
spear damage and — crucially — the unit **can't be targeted while airborne**, so it doubles as
durability. But the leap costs a turn, the landing is telegraphed (the target can simply walk out of
the spot or be healed before you land), and you cede board presence in the meantime. Strong as a safe
single-target poke, mediocre as a fight-carrier.

**Why this tier.** Safe, high single-target damage is real, but tempo-negativity and a dodgeable
landing cap it; high HP/PA and the airborne window keep it from sinking lower.

## Strengths

- **Safe burst:** Jump deals heavy single-target spear damage while the unit is untargetable mid-air —
  damage and evasion in one action.
- **Durable spear body:** HP 120, PA 120, 15% C-Ev, spear reach (strike at 2 tiles, over allies);
  Jump+ upgrades extend horizontal range and vertical reach dramatically.
- **No resource cost:** Jump needs no MP and doesn't break gear.

## Weaknesses

- **Tempo-negative and telegraphed:** the leap burns a turn, the landing can be dodged or pre-healed,
  and you give up the board while airborne.
- **Single-target only; no magic** (MP 50, MA 50) — no AoE, no flexibility.
- **Investment-gated:** reach depends on buying the Jump+ upgrades, and effectiveness leans on terrain.

## Action skills — Jump

> The command is **Jump**; the "skills" below are JP-purchased range/height upgrades that extend it.

| Skill | Effect | Design read |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Horizontal Jump +1 … +7 | Extend Jump's horizontal range | Turns Jump from adjacent-only into a long-range strike from safety. |
| Vertical Jump +2 … +8 | Extend Jump's vertical reach | Lets Jump cross big elevation — key on vertical maps. |

## Reaction / Support / Movement (cross-job value)

- **Reaction — Dragonheart (≈ Dragon Spirit: chance to gain Reraise):** auto-revive insurance on any
  unit; one of the better survivability reactions, classically Dragoon's.
- **Support — Equip Polearms:** spears (reach + damage) on another job; a niche reach enabler.
- **Movement — Jump +N (height):** extra vertical reach for traversing terrain (distinct from the
  Jump *command*).

## Build relevance

A niche primary for safe single-target burst on vertical maps, and a durable spear body. Its best
export is **Dragonheart** (auto-Reraise chance), which graft onto a key unit you can't afford to lose.
Field it for the safe-damage niche; mine the reaction.

# Dancer (Vanilla / IVC)

*Classic: Dancer · Command: Dance · Stats (mult.): HP 60 · MP 50 · Speed 100 · PA 110 · MA 95 · Move 3 · Jump 3 · C-Ev 5%* · *(female-only)*

The dark mirror of the Bard: map-wide enemy debuffs and damage that **accumulate every turn**. Park
it safely and grind the entire enemy team down from a corner.

## End-game tier: B  (cheese-tier A in long fights; weak in short ones)

**Community read.** Dance is Bardsong turned offensive: every dance hits *all enemies* regardless of
position and **stacks each turn**. Mincing Minuet chips the whole enemy team's HP, Slow Dance saps
their Speed, Polka/Heathen Frolic sink their PA/MA, Witch Hunt drains their MP — all unmissable, all
free, all ramping. It is brutal in long fights and cheese, weak in short ones. Unlike the Bard it has
real PA (110), so it can fight if needed, but it's gender-locked (female) and fragile.

**Why this tier.** A free, can't-miss, accumulating *enemy* debuff/damage is powerful given time; the
slow payoff and gender lock keep it niche, while the better body nudges it just above pure dead-weight.

## Strengths

- **Map-wide accumulating debuffs/damage:** Mincing Minuet (HP), Slow Dance (Speed), Polka/Heathen
  Frolic (PA/MA), Witch Hunt (MP) hit every enemy and grow each turn — whittle the whole side down.
- **Unmissable and free:** no MP, no targeting; the enemy can't dodge a dance.
- **Usable PA (110):** a more capable body than the Bard if it must act directly.

## Weaknesses

- **Slow payoff:** dances ramp over many turns; minimal impact in a quick fight.
- **Fragile and gender-locked (female):** HP 60, C-Ev 5%; a single tick is small.
- **Commits the unit to dancing:** while it dances it isn't doing much else.

## Action skills — Dance

> All dances are map-wide, affect all enemies, and accumulate over turns.

| Dance | Effect | Design read |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Mincing Minuet | Global HP damage | Chips the entire enemy team every turn — pure attrition. |
| Witch Hunt | Global MP drain | Starves enemy casters across the board. |
| Slow Dance | Global Speed/stat down | Cripples enemy tempo — fewer enemy turns over time. |
| Polka | Global PA down | Sinks enemy physical damage team-wide. |
| Heathen Frolic | Global MA down | Sinks enemy magic damage team-wide. |
| Forbidden Dance | Global random status | Scatters bad statuses across the enemy side. |
| Last Waltz | Map-wide finisher (instant-KO effect) | The capstone payoff once the enemy is softened. |

## Reaction / Support / Movement (cross-job value)

Dancer's exports are negligible — its value is the command, danced from a primary slot.

- **As a secondary:** **Dance** lets a safe body grind down the enemy team while contributing little
  else; that's the way to "splash" it.
- **Counter to be aware of — Earplugs:** the reaction that resists song/dance shuts a Dancer down;
  expect it on some enemies.

## Build relevance

A niche but powerful primary for long/attritional fights and cheese, classically paired with a Bard
(your team ramps up while theirs grinds down) for total board control. Field it where fights last;
skip it where they're quick. Minimal to export.

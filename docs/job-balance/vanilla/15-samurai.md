# Samurai (Vanilla / IVC)

*Classic: Samurai · Command: Iaido (Draw Out) · Stats (mult.): HP 75 · MP 90 · Speed 100 · PA 128 · MA 90 · Move 3 · Jump 3 · C-Ev 20%*

The katana drawer: spend (and risk) a katana to unleash an area effect that scales with PA and Brave
and costs no MP — devastating damage *and* elite team buffs from one command.

## End-game tier: A  (a top-tier output/utility job)

**Community read.** Samurai is rated highly because **Iaido is MP-free, area-effect, no-charge, and
PA/Brave-scaling** — and several katana don't deal damage at all, they hand the team Protect+Shell,
Haste+Regen, or an area heal *for free*. High PA, high innate evade, and a strong reaction (Blade
Grasp) round it out. The cost is the katana economy: Draw Out can break or consume the blade, and you
need a stock (gil).

**Why this tier.** One command that delivers reliable area damage and best-in-class free team buffs,
on a high-PA evasive body — extremely efficient. Only the katana dependency holds it back.

## Strengths

- **Iaido is resource-light and instant:** no MP, no charge, area effect, scaling with PA *and* Brave —
  reliable output every turn.
- **Elite free buffs:** Murasame (area heal), Kiyomori (area Protect+Shell), Masamune (area
  Haste+Regen) — team support no MP-caster matches for cost.
- **Strong combatant:** PA 128, 20% C-Ev, katana; pairs damage with one of the best physical-evade
  reactions in the game.

## Weaknesses

- **Katana economy:** Draw Out can break/consume the blade, so power is gated by owning (and replacing)
  katana — a gil and inventory tax.
- **Fragile HP (75):** leans on evade, not bulk; punished by what gets through.
- **Brave-scaled:** a low-Brave Samurai underperforms, and the best katana arrive late.

## Action skills — Iaido (Draw Out)

> Sources tag every katana uniformly; the per-katana effects below are the established FFT roles. All
> are area effects that scale with PA/Brave, cost no MP, and risk breaking the drawn katana.

| Katana | Effect | Design read |

## Reaction / Support / Movement (cross-job value)

- **Reaction — Blade Grasp (≈ Shirahadori: evade physical attacks, Brave-scaled):** negates a large
  share of *all* physical attacks; a premier survivability reaction on any high-Brave unit.
- **Support — Doublehand (two-hand a weapon for more damage):** trades the shield for a big damage
  bump; strong on dedicated attackers.
- **Support — Equip Katana:** brings katana (and, with Iaido, Draw Out) to another job.

## Build relevance

A top fielded primary (MP-free damage + free elite buffs) and an excellent donor: **Iaido** as a
secondary gives any PA body free area heals/buffs, and **Blade Grasp** is a build-defining reaction.
Field it for output-plus-support; export Blade Grasp everywhere.

# Bard (Vanilla / IVC)

*Classic: Bard · Command: Bardsong · Stats (mult.): HP 55 · MP 50 · Speed 100 · PA 30 · MA 115 · Move 3 · Jump 3 · C-Ev 5%* · *(male-only)*

The support singer: map-wide, position-independent ally buffs that **accumulate every turn**. The
Bard itself does nothing in combat — it hides and lets the whole party snowball.

## End-game tier: B  (cheese-tier A in long fights; weak in short ones)

**Community read.** Bard is defined by one mechanic: Bardsongs hit *every* ally regardless of
position and **stack a little more each turn the song plays**. Park the Bard safely, sing Battle Chant
(Brave) or Rousing Melody (Speed) or Magickal Refrain (MA) or Seraph Song (regen), and the team ramps
toward godhood for free while it sits in a corner. That makes it superb in long, attritional fights
and grind/cheese setups — and nearly useless in a quick, decisive one. It is also a non-combatant and
gender-locked (male).

**Why this tier.** A free, can't-miss, accumulating party buff is genuinely powerful given time; the
slow payoff, the dead-weight body, and the gender lock keep it niche rather than universal.

## Strengths

- **Map-wide accumulating buffs:** Battle Chant / Rousing Melody / Magickal Refrain / Seraph Song hit
  all allies and grow each turn — set once, snowball forever.
- **Free and unmissable:** no MP per tick, no targeting, no positioning — the team buff can't whiff.
- **Safe to field:** the Bard contributes from a corner, out of danger.

## Weaknesses

- **Slow payoff:** songs ramp over many turns; in short fights they barely matter.
- **Dead-weight body:** HP 55, PA 30 — a non-combatant; you're spending a roster slot on a buffer.
- **Gender-locked (male)** and fragile (C-Ev 5%) — and a single tick is small.

## Action skills — Bardsong

> All songs are map-wide, affect all allies, and accumulate over turns.

| Song | Effect | Design read |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Seraph Song / Life's Anthem | Global HP regen / heal | Passive party sustain that outlasts any single heal in a long fight. |
| Rousing Melody | Global Speed/stat up | More turns for the whole team — the tempo snowball. |
| Battle Chant | Global Bravery up | Ramps the team's physical damage and reaction rates. |
| Magickal Refrain | Global Magick up | Ramps the team's magic output. |
| Nameless Song | Global random ally buff | Cheap chance at strong statuses (Reraise/Protect/etc.) across the party. |
| Finale | Map-wide finisher (instant-KO effect) | The capstone payoff after the team is set. |

## Reaction / Support / Movement (cross-job value)

Bard's exports are negligible — its value is the command, sung from a primary slot.

- **As a secondary:** **Bardsong** lets a safe back-line body buff the whole team while doing its own
  thing; that is the main way to "splash" the Bard.
- **Counter to be aware of — Earplugs:** the reaction that resists song/dance neutralizes a Bard
  entirely; expect it on some enemies.

## Build relevance

A niche but powerful primary for long/attritional fights and cheese (a Bard pumping Speed/Brave/MA can
carry a team over time), classically paired with a Dancer for total board control. Field it where the
fight lasts; skip it where the fight is quick. Minimal to export.

# Ninja (Vanilla / IVC)

*Classic: Ninja · Command: Throw · Innate: Dual Wield · Stats (mult.): HP 70 · MP 50 · Speed 120 · PA 122 · MA 75 · Move 4 · Jump 4 · C-Ev 30%*

The dual-wielding speed demon: the fastest, most evasive, hardest-hitting physical chassis in the
game. You field it for its *body* — two weapons, top Speed, huge evade — far more than for its command.

## End-game tier: S  (consensus top-tier)

**Community read.** Ninja is the benchmark top job. **Dual Wield** is innate, so it attacks twice per
turn; it has the **highest Speed (120)**, so it acts more often than anyone; the **highest evade
(30% C-Ev)**, so it dodge-tanks despite paper HP; and Move/Jump 4 for free positioning. The result is
overwhelming sustained physical damage with built-in survivability. Its Throw command is the weak
part — what makes Ninja great is the chassis, and **Dual Wield is the best offensive support to
export**.

**Why this tier.** Double attacks × most turns × hard-to-hit = the highest reliable physical output in
the game, on a body that survives by simply not being there when the blow lands.

## Strengths

- **Dual Wield (innate):** two weapon strikes per Attack — roughly double melee damage, the core of
  its dominance.
- **Best Speed (120) + Move/Jump 4:** acts most often and goes anywhere; owns the tempo.
- **Highest evade (30% C-Ev):** a genuine dodge-tank that ignores a large fraction of physical hits.

## Weaknesses

- **Fragile:** HP 70 and light armor only — it lives on evade and on killing first; what connects hurts.
- **Throw is the weak command:** it consumes weapons for ranged damage — expensive and situational; you
  bring Ninja for its attacks, not its Throw.
- **No magic, no sustain:** MA 75, MP 50, nothing to heal or buff itself.

## Action skills — Throw

> Each skill unlocks throwing a weapon class for ranged physical damage; the thrown item is consumed.

| Skill | Effect | Design read |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Throw Shuriken / Throw Bombs | Throw a cheap consumable for ranged damage | The practical Throws — cheap ammo, decent ranged chip/finish. |
| Throw Daggers / Swords / Katana / Axes / Polearms / Poles / Books / Knight's Swords / Ninja Blades / Flails | Throw that weapon class for big ranged damage | High burst, but you *destroy* the weapon — a desperation/finisher move, not a rotation. |

## Reaction / Support / Movement (cross-job value)

Ninja is the premier offensive donor in the game.

- **Support — Dual Wield (innate):** equip two weapons on any job. The single most damage-multiplying
  support there is; lifting it onto another high-PA body is build-defining.
- **Movement — Move +2 / +3:** Ninja's natural high mobility carries to any unit.
- **Reaction — Abandon (≈ Reflexes: sharply raise evasion):** stacks with the huge C-Ev for a
  near-untouchable dodge build.

## Build relevance

A best-in-class fielded primary (dual-wield speed DPS) *and* the home of **Dual Wield**, the export
that turns any strong attacker into a double-hitter. Field Ninja to dominate the action economy; lift
Dual Wield everywhere it fits. Throw is a minor situational extra.

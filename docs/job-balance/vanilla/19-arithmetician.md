# Arithmetician (Vanilla / IVC)

*Classic: Calculator · Command: Arithmeticks (Math Skill) · Stats (mult.): HP 65 · MP 80 · Speed 50 · PA 50 · MA 70 · Move 3 · Jump 3 · C-Ev 5%*

The math caster: select a number, and every unit on the map whose Level / CT / height / EXP matches it
is hit by an eligible spell — **free, instant, no charge time, map-wide**. The most broken and most
tedious job in the game.

## End-game tier: S  (raw power) — but degenerate and miserable to pilot

**Community read.** Calculator is infamous: with a borrowed spell list (Holy, Flare, Cure…) it can
nuke or heal *every* matching unit at once, with **no MP and no charge time** — erasing the single
biggest weakness of every other caster. It is widely considered game-breaking and is routinely banned
in challenge runs. The universal caveat: it has **awful stats** (Speed 50 means it barely acts),
**friendly-fires** every matching unit including your own, and demands tedious level/CT/height
bookkeeping to pull off. A win button with homework.

**Why this tier.** At end-game its output ceiling is the highest a generic can reach — instant
free map-wide spells. Power earns the S; ergonomics and fragility are the price, not a tier demotion.

## Strengths

- **Free, instant, map-wide casting:** no MP, no charge, no targeting roll — every unit matching the
  chosen number is hit at once. It deletes the charge-time problem entirely.
- **Borrows any learned spell:** pair Arithmeticks with Holy/Flare for a full-board nuke, or with
  Cure/Raise for a full-board heal.
- **Highest output ceiling among generics:** with setup, it ends fights in one action.

## Weaknesses

- **Awful stats:** Speed 50 (it rarely gets turns), HP 65, C-Ev 5% — slow and fragile.
- **Friendly fire:** the selector hits *all* matching units, allies included — setup must avoid
  catching your own team.
- **Tedious and setup-heavy:** requires level/CT/height manipulation and live arithmetic, is slow to
  execute, and brings no spells of its own (it needs a borrowed list).

## Action skills — Arithmeticks

> These are *targeting selectors*, not spells: each fires an already-learned eligible spell, for free,
> on every unit whose chosen stat matches the number.

| Selector | Effect | Design read |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Target CT / Level / EXP / Elevation | Hit units whose CT / Level / EXP / height matches | The stat axis you exploit; height/CT are the manipulable ones. |
| Prime | Selector value = a prime number | Catches an awkward spread of units; setup-dependent. |
| Multiple of 5 / 4 / 3 | Selector value = a multiple of N | The workhorses — "Multiple of 3/4" catches large swathes of the board at once. |

## Reaction / Support / Movement (cross-job value)

- **Exports: essentially none.** Arithmetician brings no signature R/S/M; it is a primary-only gimmick.
- **The command travels, though:** carrying **Math Skill as a *secondary*** onto a faster, sturdier
  body is the classic fix for the Calculator's dreadful Speed — the optimized way to wield the math.

## Build relevance

A degenerate primary (or a **Math Skill secondary** grafted onto a better chassis) that, once set up,
can win a fight in a single free action — at the cost of fragility, friendly fire, and tedium. The
strongest ceiling and the worst ergonomics in the roster.

# Mime (Vanilla / IVC)

*Classic: Mime · Command: Mimic · Innate: Concentration + Brawler + Beastmaster · Stats (mult.): HP 140 · MP 50 · Speed 120 · PA 120 · MA 115 · Move 4 · Jump 4 · C-Ev 5%*

The copycat: elite raw stats and three free innate supports, but it **cannot equip anything** and
**cannot set abilities** — its entire game is Mimic, repeating allies' actions for free.

## End-game tier: B  (a combo piece; A/S in dedicated Mime-stack cheese)

**Community read.** Mime is a paradox — the best raw stat line in the roster (highest HP, top Speed,
high PA/MA, Move/Jump 4) and three free innate supports — wrapped around brutal restrictions: no
weapons, no armor, no accessories, no items, and no settable R/S/M or action command. Solo it's just a
bare-handed brawler. Its power is **combinatoric**: positioned behind allies, it copies their actions
for free — two Mimes echoing a big summon or a mass heal multiply that effect two- or threefold for one
unit's MP. A coordinated Mime stack is cheese-tier; a lone Mime is ordinary.

**Why this tier.** Conditional on team-building. With a setup to copy, it's among the strongest things
you can do; without one, it's a stat-stick that can't gear up.

## Strengths

- **Elite raw stats:** HP 140 (highest), Speed 120, PA 120, MA 115, Move/Jump 4 — a superb chassis on
  paper.
- **Three free innate supports:** Concentration (ignore evade), Brawler (full bare-handed damage),
  Beastmaster (monster control) — all without spending slots.
- **Mimic multiplies a team:** free copies of allies' actions; stack Mimes behind a big AoE or a mass
  heal to double or triple the output for one caster's cost.

## Weaknesses

- **Cannot equip anything or use items:** no weapon scaling, no armor mitigation — offense is Brawler
  fists, defense is raw HP only.
- **Cannot set R/S/M or an action command:** the slots are locked to its innates; it has no agency of
  its own.
- **Fully dependent on allies:** solo it does nothing special, and it must be positioned to copy the
  *right* action — fragile to disruption.

## Action skills — Mimic

| Command | Effect | Design read |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Mimic | Copy allied units' actions for free (no MP/JP) | The whole job: a force-multiplier that turns one strong action into several — and nothing at all without a team built to feed it. |

## Reaction / Support / Movement (cross-job value)

- **Exports: none.** Mimic can't be placed on another job, and the support slots are locked to the
  innate trio — you cannot mine the Mime.
- **It is a *consumer* of team design, not a donor:** its value is realized only by the allies it
  copies.

## Build relevance

A combo/cheese primary: field one or two Mimes behind your biggest-AoE or biggest-heal units to
multiply their effect, leaning on the elite stats and free Concentration/Brawler to stay relevant
between copies. Outside a team built to feed it, it's a gear-less brawler. Exports nothing.

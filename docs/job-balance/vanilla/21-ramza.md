# Ramza (Vanilla / IVC)

*Classic: Ramza (special Squire) · Command: Ramza's Squire (+ generic Fundaments) · Stats (mult.): HP 120 · MP ~100–105 · Speed 100 · PA 110 · MA 100 · Move 4 · Jump 3 · C-Ev 5–15%* · *(story-unique; Traitor-immune)*

The protagonist's special Squire: better stats than the generic, Traitor immunity, flexible equipment,
and a unique skill set crowned by **Shout** — a stacking self-buff that turns him into a one-man army.

## End-game tier: A  (a story-unique, not a roster choice)

**Community read.** Ramza is one of the strongest "jobs" in the game, with the caveat that he is a
single fixed character rather than a class you assign. **Shout** raises his Brave, PA, MA, and Speed
together for free — stack it a few turns and he out-scales everything — and he comes with elevated
stats, **Traitor immunity** (he can't be permanently charmed or turned), and good gear access. Tailwind
/ Steel / Chant add team support, and Ultima is a heavy non-elemental nuke.

**Why this tier.** Self-sufficient and always available; Shout alone makes him a carry. He's rated as a
unit, not as a generic export.

## Strengths

- **Shout:** free self-buff raising Brave + PA + MA + Speed at once; stacks across turns into
  overwhelming personal power — the best reason he carries fights.
- **Strong unique chassis:** HP 120, PA 110, MA 100, Move 4, plus **Traitor immunity** and flexible
  equipment — durable and reliable.
- **Team utility + a nuke:** Tailwind (Speed), Steel (Brave), Chant (heal) support allies; Ultima is a
  heavy non-elemental finisher.

## Weaknesses

- **Story-unique:** one fixed character — not a job you can assign across the roster or replicate.
- **Shout needs ramp turns** to pay off, and **Ultima is hard to acquire** (learned by being hit by it)
  and expensive.
- **Base utility is the shallow generic Squire kit** (Focus / Rush / Throw Stone / Salve — see
  `01-squire.md`).

## Action skills — Ramza's Squire

## Reaction / Support / Movement (cross-job value)

- **Uses the generic R/S/M pool** like any unit — no special exports of his own; **Traitor immunity** is
  an innate trait, not a portable ability.
- **He is a primary carry, not a donor:** value lives in Shout + stats, not in anything you'd mine off
  him.

## Build relevance

The protagonist: field him, stack **Shout**, and let his stats spiral out of reach while he supports
the team with Tailwind/Steel/Chant and finishes with Ultima. A fixed power unit rather than a roster
export.

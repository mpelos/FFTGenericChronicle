# Thief — EVERYDAY battle kit sim (v2). Steal = niche loot, NOT the loop (Marcelo redirect).

Script: `tmp/job-drafts/sim_thief_v2.py`. Frozen DCL placeholders. Tests SHAPE of the everyday kit:
Core Mug / Backstab / Knock Loose (battle-scoped weapon de-fang) ; Tier-2 Snatch ; Steal kept niche.

## Reads
- **Knock Loose (signature) — STRONG, matchup-shaped.** Denies a weapon-user's next weapon action
  (battle-scoped; target still moves/casts/items/punches). vs Plate Knight = +16 net tempo over
  Backstab (4.3), vs Ninja +10 → the Thief neutralizes a dangerous attacker's turn while being
  un-catchable. **DEAD vs Monk/Monster/Caster (no weapon)** and only marginal vs a low-damage Archer →
  honest matchup dependence (the wrong-pick is an all-caster/all-monster comp).
- **Not oppressive (no ration conflict).** One weapon action denied, melee-range, costs the Thief's
  turn, dead vs no-weapon → a soft tempo-tax, NOT a Disable/loss-of-control door. Full Disarm stays
  Tier-2 (only if engine-supported and sim-safe).
- **Fielding justified where Knock Loose is dead.** vs Monster/Monk the Thief still contributes as a
  durable flanker (Backstab 5.7–6.8 eff dmg/turn, survives at 33% hit) — weaker vs monsters (intended),
  not dead weight.
- **Crosses the gap vs a kiting Archer.** Move 4 + Vault + top Dodge → ~11–17 dmg taken over 2–3 turns
  to close (HP 85) then Backstabs. It closes and threatens, doesn't die crossing.
- **Snatch (Tier-2) correctly situational (J2).** vs buffed enemy: removes Haste/Protect AND grants it
  to the Thief (double tempo swing) — excellent when live; **dead vs unbuffed** → a pure-combat build
  skips it. Narrow theft (can't generate the status) → no Time/White lane conflict.
- (Survival / offense-by-facing / Vigilance unchanged from sim_thief.py SIM 1/2/5: physical-front
  dodge-tank with wide holes — flank/back/magic/focus-fire; low per-hit; loses a stand-up race for the
  intended reason.)

## Verdict
The everyday kit gives the Thief a real, fun, distinct battle role independent of the niche Steal:
**the evasive tempo skirmisher that de-fangs dangerous weapon-users (Knock Loose) and punishes exposed
facing (Backstab), un-catchable (Move/Vault/Dodge/Vigilance).** Ninja kills harder, Archer controls
range, Thief makes unsafe positioning and dangerous attackers costly. Gate PASSED.

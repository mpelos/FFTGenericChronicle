SIM 6 — JUMP reliability: the short-airtime fix vs the vanilla 'walk away' feel-bad
==========================================================================================
  Boxed-in caster (walled by its own front line, can't relocate): Jump connects p~76% (rolls Dodge), lands the delete.
  Mobile/unscreened target that gets a turn: can step off the tile -> Jump WHIFFS (counterplay intact).
  Short airtime = LESS time for the target to get a turn before landing AND a briefer untargetable window
  (a one-beat dodge, not a parking spot). Durability = PLATE, not disappearing. Two-sided, vanilla feel-bad fixed.

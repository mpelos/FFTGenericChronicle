routed through this real-code branch. This does not prove that the VM lacks a separate
Earplugs check for those families, but it proves that the visible real-code trigger cannot
by itself implement the final DCL scope of performance, speech, and morale effects.

A final implementation needs an authored action-family membership map and either native/VM
proof for `0x1C/0x1D` or a DCL effect-suppression producer. Exact action ids are preferable
to a broad formula-only immunity because the design explicitly forbids broad status immunity.

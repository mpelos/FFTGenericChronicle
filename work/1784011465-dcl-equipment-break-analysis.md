  the outer order with Basic Attack `(type=1,id=0)`, calls the universal calculation entry,
  then restores the Rend order. Repointing the ability away from `0x25` removes this nested
  provenance hazard.

  records to an ordinary authored physical/status formula. If a native break formula must be
  retained for presentation, clear result bit `0x0004` at the post-calculation boundary before
  the protected side-effect applicator; preserve or replace the numeric damage independently.
- **Live gate:** native destruction cardinality matters only for abilities intentionally left on
  the vanilla break transaction. The DCL replacement itself needs physical/status integration
  tests, not a reverse-engineering test of permanent inventory loss.

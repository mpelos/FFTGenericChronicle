## Integration boundary

- Formula `0x22` is the bounded retained-carrier subset. Its two catalog actions have fixed
  native status bundles and an unconditional builder/finalizer wrapper. The runtime keeps
  those bundles only to preserve result production and replaces every inherited packet bit
  at pre-clamp.
- Formula `0x0A` can skip the finalizer at either of two native gates. Formula `0x0B` skips
  it when its native result flag is zero. A rule evaluated only at pre-clamp therefore cannot
  turn a native miss into a DCL success: that action never reaches packet replacement.
- Forcing a shared chance to 100 globally is invalid. It would affect unauthored actions and
  still would not provide execution-only 3d6 ownership, forecast probability, or AI scoring.
- The next producer must be exact-action and per-target before the conditional finalizer. It
  must distinguish forecast, AI evaluation, and execution: forecast/AI consume the authored
  probability, while execution consumes one cached 3d6 decision. Only a successful execution
  decision may retain/stage a packet for the later native commit.

This is a provenance/output-production gate, not a packet-commit gap. No new write hook is
justified until the calc-provenance live capture classifies those three modes.

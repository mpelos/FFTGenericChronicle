## Completion rule

The DCL is not technically complete while any row is `partial-live-gated`,
`metadata-authoring`, `design-open`, `integration-missing`, or `regression-pending`.
Mechanism profiles prove components only; completion requires one canonical integrated profile
and the whole-DCL regression against player forecast, execution, AI, multi-hit/AoE, statuses,
reactions, KO/lifecycle, equipment, and every authored job/action family.
